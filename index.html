Overwatch is an online PVP game where you can face your opponents in real time. Overwatch has a very deep background and contains a lot of lore behind the game. In Overwatch, there are many different gamemodes each consisting of 10 players in a 5v5 format. One of the gamemodes being push, where you have to push a robot to its intended destination while surviving attacks from the enemy team.

Overwatch has 38 characters, or "heroes", all of these heroes have different weapons and abilities. The gameplay of these different agents can really differ depending on how you play them, adding some variety to the game. In Overwatch, there are 3 different roles, Tank, Damage and Support. All of these roles have different types of abilities and play style. for example, the heroes under the Tank role have more health and often have a shield or a damage mitigating ability, however the support role's job is to heal and help the team throughout the game, whether that means through saving your team, or doing damage.
